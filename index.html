About Pet Pride

PET PRIDE has been operating a "NO KILL"and cage-free shelter dedicated to the lifetime care of homeless cats since 1965. We welcome visitors to come see our cats living happily in their cage-free "Home for Cats," fully furnished for the cats' pleasure and enjoyment.

Programs

Foster or adopt a cat. Sponsor one of our shelter cats. Adding us to your will.

Donations

We greatly appreciate your support to ensure that the cats we serve will always have a home at our santuary. We use PayPal for donations and cat sponsorships.

We are a non-profit, tax-deductible organization under IRC Section 501(c)(3). For any donation of $250.00 or more, we will send you a receipt in compliance with the Internal Revenue Service requirements.
