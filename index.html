    About Pet Pride

    PET PRIDE, founded in 1965, owns and operates a"NO KILL"and cage-free shelter for the lifetime care of homeless cats. We welcome visitors to come see our cats living happily in their cage-free"Home for Cats," fully furnished for the cats' pleasure and enjoyment.
