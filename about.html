The oldest Non-Profit Organization for the benefit of cats

PET PRIDE, founded in 1965, owns and operates a"NO KILL"and cage-free shelter for the lifetime care of homeless cats.

We are located at

3350 S. Robertson Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90034
Our hours are 11AM-4PM daily exempting Wednesdays and Sundays.

We welcome visitors to come see our cats living happily in their cage-free"Home for Cats," fully furnished for the cats' pleasure and enjoyment.

Our healthy and purring cats are available for adoption to approved homes.Please call us for more information or appointments; our phone number is (310) 836-5427.
